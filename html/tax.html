The Structure (Big Picture)

The Indian Tax System has two engines.

üéØ

1. Direct Taxes

"The Slap on the Face"

Paid directly by you to the government. It hurts more because you see the money leaving your bank account.

  • Income Tax (On Salary/Business)
  • Capital Gains (On Stock Profits)
  • Corporate Tax (On Company Profits)
üçî

2. Indirect Taxes

"The Silent Pinch"

Collected from you, but paid by the seller. You pay this every time you consume something.

  • GST (Goods & Services Tax)
  • Customs Duty (On Imported Goods)
  • Excise Duty (On Petrol/Liquor)

Compliance: TDS & Advance Tax

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)

The government doesn't trust you to pay later. So they cut tax before paying you. Applies to Salary, Interest, Rent > ‚Çπ50k, Crypto.

Tip: Check Form 26AS to see your TDS credits.

Advance Tax (The Quarterly EMI)

If your annual tax liability > ‚Çπ10,000, you cannot wait till March. You must pay in installments.

15 June (15%) 15 Sept (45%) 15 Dec (75%) 15 Mar (100%)
