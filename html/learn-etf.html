The Secret Keeper: Authorized Participants (APs)

If everyone sells NiftyBees, price should crash, right? Wrong.

APs (Big Banks) step in. If ETF price < True Value (NAV), they buy the ETF and sell the underlying stocks for a profit. This arbitrage keeps the price glued to the true value.

LEVEL 03: PRO

Critical Metrics (Don't Ignore)

1. Tracking Error

If Nifty goes up 10%, your ETF should go up 10%. If it goes up only 9.8%, the 0.2% is Tracking Error. Lower is better.

2. Impact Cost (Liquidity)

Don't just look at "Volume". Look at the Bid-Ask Spread. If you want to buy at ‚Çπ100 but the seller is at ‚Çπ101, you lose 1% instantly. Stick to high-volume ETFs like NIFTYBEES or BANKBEES.
