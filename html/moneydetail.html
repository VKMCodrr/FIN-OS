LEVEL 05: THE MENU

5. Key Instruments (What are you buying?)

This isn't just "Debt". It's specific paper.

1. Treasury Bills (T-Bills)

Issued by Govt of India. Zero default risk.
Maturity: 91, 182, 364 Days.

2. Commercial Paper (CP)

Issued by Companies for working capital.
Risk: Depends on company rating. Higher return.

3. Certificate of Deposit (CD)

Issued by Banks. Tradable FDs essentially.
Risk: Very Low (Bank backed).

4. Call Money

Overnight lending between banks.
The pulse of the banking system.

LEVEL 06: CORE LINK

6. Interest Rates & RBI

The Transmission Channel

Money market is the first to react when RBI changes the Repo Rate.

  • üìà RBI Hikes Rate: Money market returns jump immediately.
  • üìâ RBI Cuts Rate: Money market returns drop immediately.

It is the most sensitive part of the debt market.

LEVEL 07: HOW TO BUY

7. How You Participate (Mutual Funds)

You don't buy CPs directly. You buy Funds that buy them.

Liquid Funds

Invests in 91-day papers. Safe. Good for 1-3 months parking.

Overnight Funds

Invests in 1-day papers. Safest mutual fund category. Zero risk.

Ultra Short Term

3-6 months duration. Slightly higher return, slightly higher risk.

LEVEL 13: THE SIGNAL

13. The Crisis Signal

The Canary in the Coal Mine

Before the stock market crashes, the money market usually freezes.

If banks stop lending to each other overnight (Call Money rates spike), it means Trust has broken.

Watch liquidity, not just Nifty levels.
