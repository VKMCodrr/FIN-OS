LEVEL 05: THE MENU

5. Major Categories (Indian Context)

1. Precious Metals

Gold, Silver.
Used for: Crisis protection, Inflation hedge. Emotional value in India.

2. Base Metals

Copper, Aluminum, Zinc.
Used for: Industry, Infra. These are "Economic Thermometers".

3. Energy

Crude Oil, Natural Gas.
Driven by: Geopolitics (War). Highly volatile. Affects everyone's wallet.

4. Agri Commodities

Wheat, Cotton, Sugar.
Driven by: Monsoons & Govt Policy (MSP). Highly political.

LEVEL 09: INDIA'S LOVE

9. The Gold Reality

Gold is not just an asset; it's an emotion. But treat it logically.

Gold Works Best When:

  • Interest Rates are falling.
  • There is Fear/War in the world.
  • The Currency (Rupee) is weakening.

Gold does NOTHING when the economy is booming.

LEVEL 10: WARNING

10. Risks in Commodities

No Cash Flow

Stocks give dividends. Bonds give interest. Gold sits there. It produces nothing.

Cyclicality

Commodities can stay flat for 10 years (Bear market) and then 5x in 2 years. Timing is hard.
