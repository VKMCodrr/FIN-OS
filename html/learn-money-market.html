LEVEL 02: MECHANIC

The Toolbox (T-Bills & CPs)

This market is where Banks, Governments, and Giants borrow cash for 1 to 364 days.

  • T-Bills (Treasury Bills): You lend to the Govt of India for 91, 182, or 364 days. Risk: ZERO.
  • CP (Commercial Paper): You lend to Reliance/Tata for 3 months. Risk: Low.
  • CD (Certificate of Deposit): You lend to HDFC Bank for 1 year. Risk: Low.
