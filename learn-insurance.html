What is Insurance? (Risk Transfer)

Insurance is not an investment. It is a contract where you pay a small "Service Fee" (Premium) to a massive entity, so that when a "Critical Error" (Death, Accident, Fire) occurs, they bear the financial cost instead of you.

Brutal Truth: If you are buying insurance to "get money back," you are doing it wrong. That is a marketing trap.
