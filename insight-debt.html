Chains vs Leverage

Debt is a tool. Like a knife, it can cut your food or cut your hand.

✅ Good Debt (Leverage)

Debt that puts money IN your pocket later.

  • Education Loan: Increases your salary potential.
  • Business Loan: Expands revenue.
  • Home Loan (Rental): Tenant pays the EMI.

❌ Bad Debt (Chains)

Debt that takes money OUT for things that lose value.

  • Car Loan: Car depreciates 20% instantly.
  • Credit Card EMI: Paying 18% interest on Pizza.
  • Personal Loan: Funding a wedding to impress others.
