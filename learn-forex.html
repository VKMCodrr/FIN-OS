What Moves Price? (Macro)

Forex is not about charts. It is about Central Banks.

  • Interest Rates: Higher rates attract money. If US Fed raises rates, USD goes UP.
  • Inflation: High inflation destroys currency value.
  • Geopolitics: War scares investors. They run to safe havens (USD, CHF).
