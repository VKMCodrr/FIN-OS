LEVEL 01: NOOB

Real Assets

Commodities are raw materials. Oil, Gold, Silver, Wheat.

Unlike stocks, they don't produce earnings. A bar of gold will remain one bar of gold forever. It won't give birth to a baby gold bar (Dividend).
